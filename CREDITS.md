## Credit Policy

| source | author | license | attribution status | commercial use | redistribution note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kenney asset pages | Kenney | Creative Commons CC0 / CC0 1.0 Universal | Not required; optional credit recommended. | Allowed. | APK and public repository redistribution are allowed for CC0 project-ready files when license proof is retained. |
| OpenGameArt individual CC0 pages | Listed asset author | CC0 | Not required unless the page asks for courtesy text; optional credit recommended. | Allowed. | APK and public repository redistribution are allowed when the individual page lists the downloadable file under CC0. |
| OpenGameArt CC-BY or OGA-BY pages | Listed asset author | CC-BY 4.0 or OGA-BY | Required exactly as the source page or license specifies. | Allowed only after attribution is accepted. | Public repo redistribution requires source-page proof and complete attribution. |
| Paid itch.io packs | Marketplace author | Pack-specific paid or royalty-free license | Required if the license says so. | Allowed only after license proof confirms it. | Original paid/download files stay out of Git unless the paid license expressly permits public redistribution. |

## Required Credit Format For Attributed Assets

When a CC-BY or OGA-BY asset is accepted, add one line per asset:

| asset name | author | source | license | attribution text |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Exact asset title | Exact author from source page | Permanent source URL | Exact license name and version | Exact required attribution, including modifications when required by the license. |

Do not ship an attributed asset until this file contains the complete required attribution text.
